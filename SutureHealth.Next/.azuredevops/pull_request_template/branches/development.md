## **Suture Health Development Branch P/R Guidelines**

These guidelines are for merging into the development branch. While they are less stringent than those for the main branch, it's still important to ensure quality and clarity.

**Developer Creating P/R:** Please provide a description of your changes, ensure that your changes build successfully, and list any dependencies on other repositories.

**Developer Reviewing P/R:** Offer a general review. Check the build status of the P/R and verify the feature based on the ticket acceptance criteria.

1. **Does your work build without errors?**
   - [ ] Yes
   - [x] No (please address this)
2. **What changes are being made?**
   1. _change 1 here_
   2. _change 2 here_
3. **Does your P/R depend on changes in another repository?**
   - [ ] Yes (provide a link to the corresponding changes below)
   - [x] No
